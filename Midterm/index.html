Starring Tia and Tamera Mowry as identical twins separated at birth and reunited as teenagers. After a chance meeting at a shopping mall, they discover their relationship and, along with their adoptive parents—Lisa Landry and Ray Campbell—decide to live together under one roof. The show humorously explores their contrasting personalities—Tia being the studious and responsible one, and Tamera being more free-spirited and spontaneous—as they navigate adolescence, family dynamics, and the challenges of growing up.

Sister Sister

1998 PG 2 Seasons

Sister, Sister is a classic ’90s sitcom about Tia and Tamera, identical twin sisters who were adopted by different parents and unexpectedly reunite as teenagers. After meeting by chance, the girls — one book-smart and reserved, the other fun-loving and impulsive — move in together with their adoptive parents, creating a quirky blended household. The show follows their journey through high school, friendship, family life, and sisterhood, blending heart and humor as they learn what it really means to be siblings..
